# Acme Corp Brand Guidelines v2.3
Updated: March 2024

## Colors
Our primary brand color is #3366FF (Acme Blue).
Secondary: #FF6633 (Acme Orange)
We also use:
- Light gray for backgrounds: #F5F5F5
- Dark text: #333333
- Muted text: #999999

## Typography
We use Roboto for headings and Open Sans for body copy.
Heading sizes: 32px, 24px, 18px, 16px
Body: 14px, line-height 1.5

## Buttons
Primary buttons are Acme Blue with white text, rounded corners (8px).
Secondary buttons have a border only.

## Spacing
Use 8px grid. Common values: 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64.

## Logo
The Acme wordmark should have 24px clear space on all sides.
Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo.

## Voice
- Be friendly but professional
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Short sentences preferred

## Don'ts
- Don't use Comic Sans
- Don't use more than 3 colors on a single page
- Never put text on busy backgrounds
