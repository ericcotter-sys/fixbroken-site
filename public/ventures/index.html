Build now
comply Moat: 9 $149–399/mo

SOC 2 and ISO 27001 evidence collection that runs automatically. Replaces the compliance spreadsheet.

Who pays
Security/ops leads at seed-to-Series B startups prepping for enterprise sales.
Why they stay
Can't reconstruct 6 months of compliance evidence retroactively. Their auditor depends on your export format. Switching means starting over.
Competitive gap
Vanta, Drata, Secureframe proved this at $100M+ ARR. No one offers it at $149/mo for a 12-person startup.
MVP scope
GitHub integration only. Pull branch protection, PR settings, team membership nightly. Dashboard + CSV export for auditor. 7 days.
Strongest moat on the list. The product itself practices what it sells. Build first.
ledger Moat: 8 $79–249/mo

One dashboard for all your AI API spend — OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere, Replicate — broken down by team and feature.

Who pays
Engineering managers and finance at companies burning $500–50K/mo on AI APIs.
Why they stay
6 months of cross-provider spend history can't be recreated from any single provider. Finance won't switch mid-fiscal.
Competitive gap
Helicone and Keywords AI are $500+/mo. Dead-simple $79/mo version doesn't exist.
MVP scope
OpenAI usage API only. Connect key, nightly sync, spend chart, model breakdown, Slack alert. 7 days.
Fastest to validate. AI spend is exploding, cost visibility is terrible. Build second.
census Moat: 7 $99–249/mo

Asset inventory and inspection ledger for vehicles, equipment, devices. Immutable audit trail. VUMO integration built in.

Who pays
Fleet and ops managers at 20–500 asset companies. $99/mo flat vs. competitors' per-user pricing.
Why they stay
Complete asset lifecycle history — inspections, maintenance, condition changes. Insurance and compliance audits depend on it.
Competitive gap
Fleetio, AssetPanda, UpKeep are $100+/user enterprise. No $99/mo flat-rate version for small fleets.
VUMO edge
VUMO does the walkaround. Census stores the results. That integration is the distribution wedge no competitor has.
VUMO integration makes this defensible. Build third.
Hold — revisit after top 3 ship
signal Moat: 8 $99–299/mo

LLM output quality scoring and drift alerts. High moat, but needs a design partner running LLMs in production to validate scoring accuracy. Don't build blind.

vault Moat: 7 $99–299/mo

Encrypted doc exchange with audit trail. DocSend sold for $165M. Security-first version for legal at $99/mo is a real gap. Solid, but not urgent.

ingest Moat: 7 $49–199/mo

Messy CSV to clean JSON. Flatfile raised $50M here. Risk: simple enough that someone bolts it onto existing tooling.

Skip — crowded or unproven
pulse Moat: 6 $49–149/mo

AI endpoint monitoring. Uptime monitoring is commoditized. "AI-specific" angle is unproven as a standalone product.

pipe Moat: 6 $49–149/mo

Webhook routing. Real market, five funded competitors. Zapier is good enough for most buyers.

watch Moat: 6 $49–149/mo

Web page change detection. Visualping and Distill.io own this. Hard to differentiate at the low end.

intake Moat: 5 $79–199/mo

Smart forms. Typeform and Jotform own this. Validation is a feature, not a product. Lowest conviction on the list.
